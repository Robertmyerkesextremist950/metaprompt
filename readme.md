## 📌 About this project

Metaprompt helps you edit and refine your AI chat experiences. Large Language Models often require specific instructions to produce the best results. This tool acts as a layer between your input and the AI. It structures your thoughts into clear, organized prompts. By using this software, you simplify the way you interact with complex AI models. You gain more control over the output and reduce the time spent on manual corrections.

## 💻 System requirements

Before you install this software, confirm your computer meets these minimum standards:

*   Processor: A processor with at least 2.0 GHz speed.
*   Memory: 8 GB of RAM or higher.
*   Storage: 500 MB of available space on your hard drive.

## 🚀 Setting up the application

Once you launch the app for the first time, you need to configure your settings:

1. Double-click the metaprompt icon on your desktop.
2. The main dashboard appears. This area shows your chat history and active projects.
3. Locate the settings menu icon in the top right corner.
4. Enter your preferred AI service API key in the designated box. This key allows the software to speak with the AI model. 
5. Select your default model from the drop-down menu. 
6. Click "Save" to commit your settings.

## 🛠️ Using metaprompt features

Metaprompt offers several tools to help your workflows. 

### Chat organization
The dashboard tracks every conversation. You can rename chats or move them into folders based on your topics. This keeps your workspace clean.

### Composable prompts
You can save segments of text that you use frequently. When you start a new conversation, insert these segments to set the context immediately. This removes the need to type redundant instructions.

### Automatic refinement
The software includes a tool that reviews your prompt before you send it to the AI. It suggests changes to structure and clarity. Review these suggestions and apply them with a single click.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

### Does my data stay private?
Yes. Your conversations remain stored locally on your machine. You only send data to the AI service when you explicitly click the button to generate a response.

### Can I use this for work?
Many users employ this tool to standardize brand voice and maintain consistency across team chats. It works well for individual tasks and complex collaborative projects.

### What if the app fails to start?
Restart your computer. If the issue persists, download the installer again and run it as an administrator by right-clicking the file and choosing "Run as administrator."

### Does it require a paid subscription?
The software is free to use. However, some AI services require their own paid keys or accounts to function.
